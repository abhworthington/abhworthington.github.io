Alton B.H. Worthington

I'm a PhD Candidate in Political Science at the University of Michigan. My main research interests focus on issues of comparative and international political economy (especially the evolution of non-tariff barriers to trade), although I've been known to collaborate and work in many more areas.

My dissertation project explains the shift in protectionist policies from tariffs (and tariff-like policies) to other (non-tariff) measures. It's part of my larger research agenda linking the politics of capital mobility to the politics of goods and services market openness.

This site is so sparse as to be embarassing, but rest assured that more is on the way. (2016-09-08, ABHW)
